[Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

Touched entries carry a `tags: [cairn-1e-<lang>]` marker so the
attribution is traceable at the data layer.

Sources:
- `cairn-srd-ca.md` — Catalan SRD (`~/src/docs/cairn/cairn-ca/`)
- `cairn-srd-es.md` — Spanish SRD (`~/src/docs/cairn/`)

pre-existing Knave-derived NHC content and is **not** sourced from
Cairn.
